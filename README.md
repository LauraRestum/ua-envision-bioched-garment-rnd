# UA x Envision Bio/Chem Garment R&D

A structured briefing and supporting materials for a defense-funded R&D partnership between Under Armour and Envision, targeting next-generation bio/chemical protective garments.

## System Model

| Partner | Role |
|---|---|
| Under Armour | Advanced material innovation — develops the materials |
| Envision | Rapid prototyping + garment integration — transitions materials to deployable systems |
| DEVCOM / Government | Funds and evaluates through OTA and related pathways |

## Purpose

Present a believable, executable pathway from material innovation to deployable garment system. Sales-led. Not marketing.
