# Envision

## Positioning

Envision is a transition partner — the bridge between R&D and deployment.

Not a research lab. Not a commodity manufacturer. The specific function Envision fills is taking advanced materials and integrating them into functional, testable, manufacturable garment systems at speed.

## Capabilities

- Rapid cut & sew prototyping
- Garment development
- Small-batch pilot manufacturing
- Textile system integration

## Role in This Model

Envision sits between UA's material output and the government's evaluation process.

When UA produces an advanced material, Envision:
1. Integrates it into a full garment system
2. Produces functional prototypes for testing
3. Iterates rapidly based on evaluation feedback
4. Supports scale-up to pilot manufacturing

This compresses what typically takes months into a much shorter cycle — and does it in a way the government can evaluate, fund, and eventually procure.

## What This Partnership Unlocks for Envision

- Access to advanced materials from a recognized performance brand
- Defense funding and evaluation pathway via OTA
- A repeatable model applicable across soldier system categories
