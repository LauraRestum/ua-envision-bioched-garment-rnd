# Under Armour

> Note: The capabilities below are assumed based on public knowledge of UA's R&D direction — not sourced directly from UA. These assumptions are subject to challenge and should be validated in any live engagement.

## Capabilities

- Advanced material innovation
- Performance textile R&D
- Thermal regulation systems
- Antimicrobial fabric development
- Performance layering and wearable system integration

## Role in This Model

Under Armour develops the materials. They do not own the pipeline from material to deployable garment system — that transition is the gap this model is designed to close.

In this partnership, UA is the upstream node: their material R&D feeds into Envision's rapid prototyping and garment integration work.

## What This Partnership Unlocks for UA

- A defined pathway from material R&D output to prototype and evaluation
- Defense-funded validation of performance textiles in a high-requirement environment
- A speed-to-prototype capability they don't currently have in-house
